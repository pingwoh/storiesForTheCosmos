Greek Representations in Media

Hercules

Disney movie adaptation about Hercules. Produced in 1997, this adaptation modernizes the myth of Hercules is said to go 'to great lengths to spice up its mythic materials with wacky comedy and cheerfully anachronistic dialogue.

Lore Olympus

Original Webtoon by Rachel Smythe. This comic heavily focuses on Persephone and her relationship with the surrounding gods and goddesses.

Frankenstein

Book adaptation by Mary Shelly. This book draws strong inspiration from the story of Prometheus and creates 'metaphorical parallels.'

Hades

Video Game adaptation by Supergiant Games. This game, released in 2020, incorporates gods and other figures in their narrative of a 'dysfunctional family.'

Battlestar Galactica

Television adaption by Glen A. Larson. This franchise from the 1980s developed and created concepts based on Classic Mythology. For example many ship names in the Fleet reflect the names of Gods and Goddesses.

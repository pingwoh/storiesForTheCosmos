Stories for the Cosmos will explore the popular Greek mythology figures such as Greek Gods and Goddesses like Zeus, Posiedon and Athena. It will also portray distinct constellations and highlight the stories behind them. There is also a page to briefly go over the Greek history as well as a page that points the various adaptations of Greek mythology in movies, books, and comics.

Signed by Angelo Vergara, Yoonjoo Park, Katherine Byers, and Sam Pomajevich on 03/10/2021
